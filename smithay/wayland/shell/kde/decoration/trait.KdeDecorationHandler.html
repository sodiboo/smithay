fn request_mode( &mut self, _surface: &WlSurface, decoration: &OrgKdeKwinServerDecoration, mode: WEnum<Mode>, )

Handle surface decoration mode requests.

Called when a surface requests a specific decoration mode or acknowledged the compositor’s decoration request.

It is up to the compositor to prevent feedback loops, a client is free to ignore modes suggested by [OrgKdeKwinServerDecoration::mode] and instead request their preferred mode instead.
